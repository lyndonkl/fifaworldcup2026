The unit

One dot is money that actually moved: a fixed grain of matched notional at population scale, one trade at zoom scale. Grain changes are narrated on screen, never silent. Dots never stand for scores, statistics, or probabilities; derived quantities are drawn as outline marks above the population.

Data

Tick-level trade history and one-minute candlesticks for every World Cup-related Kalshi market, via the public API; benchmarks from Polymarket minute history, a professional book, published poll waves, and published simulation snapshots. Raw store and reproducible pipeline described in the repository.
